Climate Change

Climate change is a global challenge caused by the accumulation of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere...

Climate change causes extreme weather, sea level rise, and affects biodiversity.

Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances or products into the environment...

Pollution can be air, water, or land pollution, and it has severe health consequences.

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is energy that comes from natural resources that are constantly replenished...

Solar, wind, and hydropower are examples of renewable energy sources.

Conservation

Conservation is the protection and preservation of the natural environment...

Conserving resources helps ensure a sustainable future for generations to come.
